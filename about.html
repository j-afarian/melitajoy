About the Author

Melita Joy's life has always been eventful. Having ridden wonderful highs and suffered tremendous setbacks, her outlook has always been: I'll write about this one day! The creation of Gisella was cathartic in providing the opportunity to explore topics close to Melita's heart.

When she is not writing, she loves spending time with her husband--whom she calls Mr. Romance himself--and their six adult children. Melita and her family reside in Sydney, Australia.

Please visit her Facebook page, the more, the merrier and feel free to have a conversation with Melita. She loves to chat almost as much as she loves to write.

Happy reading,
Melita Joy
